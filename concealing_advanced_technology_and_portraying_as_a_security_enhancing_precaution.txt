Numerous groups as part of their official government policies have attempted to cover up the existence of advanced technology on this instance of this planet, because it is located in the original universe, in order to make it less interesting to random observers and therefore improve security conditions and reduce criminal attentions. Contrary to being an indication of being a convicted criminal, not perceiving advanced technology on this instance of this planet is in fact generally a normal and widespread condition, due to those types of efforts.
