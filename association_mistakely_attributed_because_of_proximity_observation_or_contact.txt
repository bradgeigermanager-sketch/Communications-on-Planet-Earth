Maneuvering or manipulating criminals or enemies or their assets into an area so that they can be collected or detected or destroyed or arrested can lead to others in such an area being misidentified as criminals, either because of criminals observing them and those who observe the criminals or enemies believe they are observing their associates, or because of contacts from the criminals or enemies that are believed to indicate association with those contacted, or simply because of locational proximity leading observers to believe that all in the general location are criminals or enemies.
